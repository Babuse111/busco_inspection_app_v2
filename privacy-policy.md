# Privacy Policy for Buscor Inspection App

**Last Updated: November 5, 2025**

## Introduction

Buscor ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Buscor Inspection mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Employee ID and authentication credentials
- Full name and role within Buscor
- Contact information (email address)

### Inspection Data
- Bus inspection checklists and results
- Photos taken during inspections
- GPS location data during inspections
- Timestamps and inspection metadata
- Notes and comments added to inspection items

### Device Information
- Device type and operating system
- App usage analytics and crash reports
- Camera and photo permissions

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **Safety Compliance**: Process inspection data to ensure bus safety standards
- **Fleet Management**: Monitor vehicle conditions and maintenance needs
- **Record Keeping**: Maintain inspection records for regulatory compliance
- **User Authentication**: Verify employee identity and role-based access

### Secondary Uses
- **App Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality
- **Support Services**: Provide technical assistance and customer support
- **Legal Compliance**: Meet regulatory requirements for transportation safety

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

### Internal Sharing
- Inspection data is shared with Buscor fleet managers and maintenance teams
- Safety-critical issues are immediately escalated to relevant personnel
- Aggregated data may be used for operational reporting

### Third-Party Services
- **Supabase**: Cloud database hosting for secure data storage
- **Expo**: Mobile app development and distribution platform
- **Google Play Services**: App distribution and updates

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by law, regulation, or legal process.

## Data Security

### Protection Measures
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication with role-based access controls
- Regular security audits and monitoring
- Cloud infrastructure with enterprise-grade security

### Photo Storage
- Inspection photos are uploaded to secure cloud storage
- Access is restricted to authorized Buscor personnel
- Photos are retained according to company policy

## Data Retention

- Inspection records are retained for regulatory compliance periods
- Personal information is kept only as long as necessary
- Data deletion requests will be honored where legally permissible

## Your Rights

### Access and Control
- View your inspection history within the app
- Update your profile information
- Request data deletion (subject to business requirements)
