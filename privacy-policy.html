Privacy Policy for Buscor Bus Inspection App

Effective Date: November 7, 2025

Last Updated: November 7, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how Buscor Bus Inspection App ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

Camera Permission Usage

Our app requests android.permission.CAMERA to:

  • Capture photos during bus inspections
  • Document inspection findings and maintenance issues
  • Create visual records for safety compliance reports
  • Attach photographic evidence to inspection checklists

Your camera access is used solely for inspection documentation purposes and photos remain under your control.

Information We Collect

Camera and Photo Data

  • Photos taken during bus inspections
  • Visual documentation of vehicle conditions
  • Images of safety issues or maintenance requirements

Location Information

  • GPS coordinates of inspection locations
  • Location context for inspection reports
  • Tracking where inspections are performed

Inspection Data

  • Inspection forms and safety checklists
  • Driver/mechanic identification
  • Bus information and maintenance records
  • Date, time, and duration of inspections
  • Notes and comments on inspection items

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Facilitate bus inspection processes - Enable comprehensive safety inspections
  • Generate inspection reports - Create detailed documentation for compliance
  • Maintain vehicle records - Track maintenance history and safety status
  • Ensure safety compliance - Meet transportation safety regulations
  • Improve services - Enhance inspection processes and app functionality

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely using encrypted cloud storage (Supabase)
  • Camera photos are only used for inspection documentation
  • Location data is used solely for inspection location tracking
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures
  • Data access is limited to authorized personnel only

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To authorized personnel for inspection review and fleet management
  • With your explicit consent
  • For safety-critical issues that require immediate escalation

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data and inspection records
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Revoke permissions through your device settings

Permission Management

Camera Permission: You can revoke camera access in your device settings under App Permissions. Note that disabling camera access will prevent you from taking inspection photos.

Location Permission: Location access can be managed in your device settings. Disabling location may limit inspection location tracking features.

Data Retention

  • Inspection data is retained for regulatory compliance periods
  • Photos are stored as long as associated inspection records are maintained
  • Personal data is deleted when no longer necessary for business purposes
  • You may request earlier deletion subject to legal and safety requirements

Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for use by authorized Buscor personnel only and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify users of material changes through the app or other appropriate means.
